3. In Undertale, there's a secret ending called "The hacker ending". This can be achieved by modifying the game's code. In this particular ending, Sans, one of the key characters of the main plot, will say something special. He will call you on your cellphone, as he normally does in the neutral ending, but near the end of the conversation there will be a twist. Sans's eyes will turn totally black, and he will say: "You're just a dirty hacker, aren't you?". After this, he will suddently close the call, leaving you to the credits.
